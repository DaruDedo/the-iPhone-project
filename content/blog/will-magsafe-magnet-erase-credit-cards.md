It's a common fear: will putting your credit cards next to a strong magnet erase their magnetic stripes? Does magsafe damage credit cards?

Here is the truth: Modern credit cards use high-coercivity (HiCo) magnetic strips and EMV chips. These are highly resistant to standard magnetic fields. 

Furthermore, premium MagSafe wallets feature custom steel shielding on the back side. This blocks the magnetic field from passing into the card slots, ensuring your cards remain 100% safe and functional.
