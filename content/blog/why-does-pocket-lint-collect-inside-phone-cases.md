Pocket lint and dust collect inside phone cases because cotton pockets shed micro-fibers, which enter the case openings. Cheap silicone cases attract this lint due to static charge.

To prevent this:
1. Turn your pockets inside out to clean out lint.
2. Switch to non-static polycarbonate cases that don't attract dust.
