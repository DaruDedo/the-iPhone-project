Your phone goes everywhere with you, collecting grease, dust, and bacteria. We recommend:

* **Daily**: Wipe the screen with a clean microfiber cloth.
* **Weekly**: Remove the case, wash the cover with mild soap and water, and wipe down the phone frame to remove trapped lint.
