Cheap screen protectors have sharp, straight edges that chip easily and scrape against your fingers during swipe gestures. That is why checking for 2.5d tempered glass vs 3d styles is essential.

A 2.5D curved tempered glass features polished, rounded borders. These rounded borders blend seamlessly with the iPhone’s curved screen edges, preventing edge chipping and ensuring a smooth touch feel during swiping gestures.
