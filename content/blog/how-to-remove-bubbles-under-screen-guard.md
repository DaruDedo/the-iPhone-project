If you have tiny bubbles under your newly installed screen guard, don't panic. You can easily remove bubbles screen protector issues without discarding the glass:

* **Air Bubbles**: Use a microfiber cloth wrapped around a credit card and push the bubble firmly towards the nearest edge.
* **Dust Bubbles**: Gently lift the nearest corner of the glass using your fingernail. Use a piece of sticky tape to grab the dust particle from the screen, then let the glass snap back down.
