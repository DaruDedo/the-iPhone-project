When a phone hits concrete, it stops instantly. This rapid deceleration creates a massive force. How do shockproof cases work to protect the internal motherboard?

Shockproof cases utilize elastic damping. By using materials that compress upon impact, the case extends the time of deceleration. A longer impact duration dramatically reduces the peak force transferred to the phone, preventing structural damage.
