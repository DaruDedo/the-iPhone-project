Cheap phone cases mold the buttons directly into the side bumper, making them stiff and mushy. A proper clicky buttons case design uses independent button inserts.

By cutting out the bumper frame and inserting separate metal or hard plastic button keys, the case acts as a physical pivot, ensuring a crisp, tactile button click.
