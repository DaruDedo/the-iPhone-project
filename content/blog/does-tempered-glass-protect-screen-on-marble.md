In Indian households with marble or tiled floors, phone drops are highly dangerous. Tiled surfaces do not flex, transferring 100% of the drop energy directly into the phone. How does screen guard protect screen layers work in these drops?

Tempered glass acts as a sacrificial shield. The chemically-strengthened glass layer absorbs and distributes the shock waves across its surface, cracking itself to save your actual OLED display panel underneath.
