With StandBy Mode in iOS 17 and 18, placing your iPhone horizontally on your desk turns it into a useful smart display. A quality magsafe stand desk accessory is perfect for this.

A good stand should feature a heavy base so it doesn't slide, an adjustable angle, and a soft silicone magnetic face to prevent case scratches.
