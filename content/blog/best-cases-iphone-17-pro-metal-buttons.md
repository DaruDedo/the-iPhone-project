A premium smartphone like the iPhone 17 Pro deserves button clicks that feel sharp and clicky. Cheap cases with molded plastic overlays make buttons mushy and frustrating. That is why an iphone 17 pro tactile case with real metal buttons is essential.

Aluminum tactile buttons are separate components inserted into the TPU frame. They wiggle slightly to replicate native device buttons, delivering an immediate response.

At The iPhone Project, our premium series features CNC-machined metal buttons color-matched to the device frame. They look luxury, protect the button recesses from dust, and preserve that satisfying click.
