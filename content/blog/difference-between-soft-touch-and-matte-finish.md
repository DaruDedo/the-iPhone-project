When comparing soft touch vs matte case textures, the surface finish determines the in-hand feel.

* **Soft-Touch**: A rubberized spray coating that feels velvety and soft. However, it can wear off over months.
* **Matte Finish**: A sandblasted texture molded directly into the plastic. It is highly durable and resists scratches and fingerprint smudges.
