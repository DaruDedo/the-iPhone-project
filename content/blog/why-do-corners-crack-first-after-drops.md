When a phone drops, it rarely lands flat. Physics dictates that it rotates, landing on a corner first. Corner impacts concentrate 100% of the kinetic energy into a single point, causing the glass to shatter.

To protect these vulnerability points, shockproof cases use thickened TPU corner bumpers with internal air pockets that compress, absorbing and distributing the impact force.
