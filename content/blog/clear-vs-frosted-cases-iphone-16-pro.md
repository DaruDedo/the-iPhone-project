When buying a case for your iPhone 16 Pro, the biggest style choice is choosing between a clear case vs frosted cover. Both look premium, but they suit different personalities.

* **The Clear Case (Glossy & Raw)**: Clear cases offer maximum visibility. They show off the raw titanium coloring and logo perfectly. However, clear cases can be prone to fingerprint smudges and require regular wiping.
* **The Frosted Case (Satin & Sleek)**: Frosted cases use a translucent matte backplate. They reduce glare, hide fingerprints, and offer a soft touch feel. The gold or gray phone colors glow softly through the frosted panel for a luxury appearance.
