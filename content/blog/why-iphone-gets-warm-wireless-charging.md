It is completely normal for your phone to get warm during charging. However, excessive heat degrades your battery health. What causes iphone heating wireless charging?

Inductive charging generates heat when the magnetic coils are misaligned or when a thick case traps heat. 

To keep your phone cool:
1. **Use MagSafe**: It ensures perfect coil alignment, reducing energy waste.
2. **Choose Polycarbonate**: Polycarbonate cases dissipate heat much faster than thick rubber or leather covers.
