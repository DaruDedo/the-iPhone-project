The Action Button and Camera Control on the iPhone 16 series are amazing features. However, many cheap phone cases render these buttons hard to press, ruining the tactile response.

For a great experience, you need an iphone 16 cover clicky buttons layout. Premium cases utilize independent, metallic button overlays. These button overlays act as physical levers that transfer pressure directly to the phone's internal switch, delivering a clean tactile "click".

At The iPhone Project, all buttons on our clear and frosted covers are CNC-machined from premium lightweight aluminum. They require zero extra effort to press and feel just as responsive as the native titanium buttons of the device.
