Buying a premium iPhone is a big investment. Keeping it looking brand new for 3 years is key to enjoying the device and retaining high resale value. 

Here are the top three maintenance rules:
1. **Always use 8K Tempered Glass**: Protects the Ceramic Shield screen from micro-scratches.
2. **Use cases with microfiber lining**: Soft internal linings prevent dust from scratching the back glass.
3. **Clean weekly**: Remove the case and wipe off any accumulated pocket lint and dust.
