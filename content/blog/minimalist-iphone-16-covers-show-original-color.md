Apple did a fantastic job with the colors of the iPhone 16 series. Hiding those vibrant colors under a solid, opaque cover feels like a waste. A minimalist clear case is the perfect answer.

Our transparent shells are made from optically clear polycarbonate that has been treated with anti-yellowing compounds. They are only 1.0mm thick, letting you appreciate the original color finish while protecting the phone from keys, pocket coins, and desk scratches.
