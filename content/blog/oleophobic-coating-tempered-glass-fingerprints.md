Have you ever noticed how some screen guards get covered in greasy fingerprints and smudges within minutes of use? This happens when a screen protector lacks an oleophobic coating.

An anti fingerprint screen guard features a microscopic layer of fluoropolymer material that repels oil and sweat. This makes it incredibly easy to wipe away smudges with a single swipe of a cloth.
