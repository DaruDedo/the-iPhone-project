Some users claim that MagSafe charging degrades battery life faster than cable charging. Does magsafe ruin battery health?

The short answer is: No. The battery degradation is caused by heat, not the magnetic charging itself. 

Using a certified MagSafe charger that aligns perfectly reduces charging heat, preserving your battery health just as well as a standard cable.
