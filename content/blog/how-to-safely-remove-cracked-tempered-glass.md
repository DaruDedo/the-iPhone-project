Removing a shattered screen protector can be tricky. Sharp glass shards can cut your fingers or scratch the front glass of your phone if done incorrectly. 

Here is our safe how to remove tempered glass guide:
1. **Use a Hairdryer**: Gently warm the screen for 10-15 seconds to soften the adhesive.
2. **Use a Plastic Card**: Slowly slide a thin plastic card (like an old ID card) under one corner of the glass.
3. **Lift Evenly**: Gently slide the card across to lift the glass panel off in one piece.
