It is highly annoying when your fresh clear case turns a dirty yellow-brown color. What is the yellow clear case solution?

The yellowing is caused by a chemical reaction when cheap TPU plastic is exposed to UV light (from the sun) and oils from your sweaty palms. 

To solve this, look for clear cases that use a rigid polycarbonate backplate combined with UV-stabilized aliphatic TPU bumpers, which resist oxidation and stay clean much longer.
