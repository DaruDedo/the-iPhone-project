When shopping for a premium iPhone case, you will constantly see brands like Spigen and ESR. How do they compare against local Indian brands like The iPhone Project?

* **Spigen**: Famous for heavy-duty shock absorption, but their designs can look bulky and expensive.
* **ESR**: Known for useful kickstand cases, but their clear backplates can scratch easily.
* **Local Premium Brands**: Match the N52 magnetic strength and hybrid drop safety of global brands, but manufacture locally in India, saving you import duties and markups.

For everyday use, local premium brands offer matching specs and sleek aesthetics at a much more reasonable price.
