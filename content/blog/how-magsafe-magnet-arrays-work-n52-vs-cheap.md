Many cheap cases claim MagSafe support, but their magnets are weak. This is because they use cheap rubber-iron composite magnets instead of a premium n52 neodymium magsafe magnet array.

N52 Neodymium is the highest grade of rare-earth magnets available. They provide massive magnetic pull force in an ultra-thin profile. 

At The iPhone Project, we embed 36 individual N52 magnets inside a copper alignment frame. This keeps your phone securely locked to car mounts, desk stands, and wallets even over major road bumps.
