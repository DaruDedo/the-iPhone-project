Installing a screen protector at home can be nerve-wracking. A single speck of dust can create an annoying bubble right in the middle of your screen. 

Here is our step-by-step guide for a bubble free tempered glass install:
1. **Find a Clean Room**: Turn off any fans and install in a clean room (like a bathroom after a hot shower, where steam has settled the airborne dust).
2. **Clean Thoroughly**: Use the wet alcohol wipe, followed by the microfiber cloth.
3. **Use the Dust Absorber**: Dab the sticky dust absorber sticker across the entire screen to catch any microscopic lint.
4. **Align and Drop**: Peel the backing film and align the glass to the speaker grill. Let it drop and watch the air push out automatically.

Using our static-absorption glass protectors makes bubble-free installation incredibly simple.
