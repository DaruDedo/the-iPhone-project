Gym workouts involve metal weights, sweaty hands, and hard tiled floors. If you use your phone to track workouts, you need a gym-friendly case.

Choose cases with a matte sweat-proof texture that won't slip from sweaty hands. Make sure it has strong N52 magnets so you can snap your phone directly to metal gym racks to record your routines.
