If you have a favorite non-MagSafe case, you can easily upgrade it using a diy magsafe ring sticker pack. These rings feature pre-applied adhesive and stick directly to the back of your case.

To install it perfectly:
1. **Clean the surface** with alcohol to remove oils.
2. **Align precisely** using the included paper template (centered around the Apple logo).
3. **Press firmly** and let the adhesive cure for 2 hours before mounting.
