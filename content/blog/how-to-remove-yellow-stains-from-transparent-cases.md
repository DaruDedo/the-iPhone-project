Many users try using baking soda, toothpaste, or alcohol to clean yellowed TPU cases. Here is the hard chemical truth: you cannot reverse yellowing once it starts.

The yellowing is an oxidation reaction inside the plastic itself. Cleaning only removes surface dirt. To avoid yellowing, buy clear cases with polycarbonate backplates and treated aliphatic TPU bumpers.
