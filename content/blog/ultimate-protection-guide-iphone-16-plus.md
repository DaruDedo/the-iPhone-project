The iPhone 16 Plus offers a gorgeous, large display and long battery life. But because it has a larger physical frame, it is also more vulnerable to frame bending and corner drops than smaller models. Choosing quality iphone 16 plus covers India options is key.

When buying a cover for the 16 Plus, prioritize:
1. **Corner Protection**: Reinforced bumpers that prevent the display from flexing on drop impact.
2. **Bezel Lips**: A raised rim of at least 1.1mm to protect that expansive Ceramic Shield screen when placed face down.
3. **Slim Profile**: A large phone is already bulky, so the case must be thin enough to maintain single-handed usability.
