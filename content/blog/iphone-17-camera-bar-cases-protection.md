The rumored horizontal camera bar design on the upcoming iPhone 17 series completely changes phone case engineering. With lenses spreading across the top edge, a simple camera ring cutout is no longer enough. You need a dedicated iphone 17 camera protector case layout.

If you place your phone on a table, the camera bar creates a pivot point. Without a case, the camera lens glass makes direct contact with surfaces, leading to micro-scratches. 

To solve this, our new cases feature a raised perimeter border around the camera bar. We use dual-material shock absorption pads inside the bumper, ensuring that flat impacts are dispersed safely around the camera array.
