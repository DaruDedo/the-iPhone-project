Many users damage their phones through simple, daily habits. The most common mistakes include:

* **Placing the phone face down without a raised lip case**: Leading to micro-scratches on the screen.
* **Charging with thick cases**: Trapping heat and degrading battery health.
* **Ignoring dust buildup inside the case**: Leading to permanent frame pitting.

Avoid these habits to keep your iPhone running and looking brand new.
