Over weeks of daily use, phone cases collect grease, dust, and lint around the edges and camera cutouts. Here is our simple how to clean phone case guide:

1. Remove the case from your phone.
2. Mix mild dish soap in warm water.
3. Use a soft toothbrush to gently scrub the bumper corners and camera lip.
4. Rinse with clean water and dry completely with a microfiber cloth before snapping it back on.
