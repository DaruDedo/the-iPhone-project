If your MagSafe card wallet slips off when you slide your phone into your pocket, the magnetic connection is too weak. This is caused by:

* **Thick non-magnetic cases**: Blocking magnetic induction.
* **Weak case magnets**: Lacking N52 neodymium pull strength.

Use cases with high-grade N52 magnet arrays to ensure a secure, slide-proof connection.
