Our fingers naturally produce sebum oil. On a normal plastic case, this oil creates a greasy, slippery layer that attracts dust. An oleophobic coating phone cover solves this.

The oleophobic layer is a microscopic fluoropolymer coating that repels oils and liquids. Smudges cannot stick to the surface, allowing you to wipe the case clean with a simple wipe.
