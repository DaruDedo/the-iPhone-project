If you remove your case and notice permanent ring marks or scuffs on the frame, it is caused by hard plastic edges rubbing against the metal rails during daily use.

Choose cases with soft internal TPU bumpers or microfiber linings to prevent friction scuffs, keeping your phone frame looking pristine.
