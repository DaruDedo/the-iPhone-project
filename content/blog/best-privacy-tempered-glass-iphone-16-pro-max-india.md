If you use your iPhone 16 Pro Max on crowded metro rides or in office meetings in India, keeping your chats private is a challenge. A premium privacy glass screen guard is the perfect solution.

Privacy screen guards utilize micro-louver technology. This technology restricts the viewing angles of your screen to 28 degrees or less. From the front, the display looks bright and clear, but anyone looking from the side only sees a black screen.

Our premium privacy tempered glass combines 9H scratch protection with high-clarity viewing filters that do not distort screen colors.
