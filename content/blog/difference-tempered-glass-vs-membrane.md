Should you choose a hard tempered glass or a flexible membrane protector? Let's compare tempered glass vs membrane styles.

* **Tempered Glass**: Made of real glass, offering high impact protection and a smooth touch feel.
* **Membrane (TPU/PET)**: A thin plastic film that is highly flexible and scratch-resistant, but offers very little drop protection.
