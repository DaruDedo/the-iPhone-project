Over time, cheap clear cases develop a greasy, sticky feeling that soapy water cannot clean. This is caused by tpu plastic degradation sticky chemical changes.

TPU degrades when exposed to UV light and sweat, breaking down the polymer chains and releasing plasticizers to the surface. 

To avoid this, choose premium polycarbonate backplates and treated aliphatic TPU bumpers.
