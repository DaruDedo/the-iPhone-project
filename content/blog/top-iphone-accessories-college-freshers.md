Starting college is exciting, and your phone will be your main hub for schedules, group chats, and taking photos. To survive campus life, college freshers need a few essential tech upgrades.

First is a reliable shockproof case with reinforced corners for accidental drops. Second is a sleek MagSafe card wallet to carry college IDs and hostel cards, and third is an 8K screen guard to protect against backpack scratches.
