Holi is a festival of colors, but water and fine organic powders can ruin speaker grills, microphones, and charging ports. To survive Holi:

Keep your phone inside a sealed waterproof pouch. Avoid taking it out to snap photos with wet hands, and use speaker meshes and case covers to block any fine powder entry points.
