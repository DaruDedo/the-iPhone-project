When comparing leather vs silicone MagSafe wallets, the material friction plays a major role in slide resistance. Let's do a magsafe wallet material comparison.

* **Silicone**: Features high surface friction, preventing the wallet from sliding sideways when slipped into tight pockets.
* **Leather**: Offers a smoother slide, which feels premium but can slide off more easily if the case backplate is slippery.
