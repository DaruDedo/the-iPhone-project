Polished metal rails (whether aluminum or titanium) are prone to micro-scratches. These scratches are often caused by dust particles trapped inside your case.

To prevent micro-scratches:
1. Choose cases with soft microfiber linings that trap dust.
2. Avoid using cases made of hard, rigid plastic that scrape the frame during installation.
