When picking a new look for your phone, the debate between a smoke cover vs clear cover is always hot. Both offer unique aesthetics.

* **Bhai Clear Case**: If you want to show off the exact titanium colors of your phone, clear is the way to go. It looks glossy, glassy, and completely raw.
* **Smoke Case (Frosted Tint)**: A smoke case features a translucent dark grey matte panel. It gives the phone a stealthy, satin, executive look that completely repels fingerprint smudges.

At The iPhone Project, we design both cases with premium anti-yellowing compounds so they stay looking brand new for months.
