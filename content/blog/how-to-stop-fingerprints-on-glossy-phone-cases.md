Glossy clear cases look gorgeous when fresh out of the box, but they become greasy fingerprint magnets within minutes of use. 

To stop fingerprints, look for clear cases with dynamic oleophobic nano-coatings that repel grease. Alternatively, switch to frosted matte cases, which diffuse light and completely hide fingerprint smudges.
