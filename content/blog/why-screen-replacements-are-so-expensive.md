Replacing a broken iPhone screen at an authorized Apple service center can cost up to 30,000 INR. This is because modern iPhone screens are highly advanced OLED panels integrated with digitizers, Face ID sensors, and Ceramic Shield glass. Using an 8K tempered glass screen guard is a smart insurance policy.
