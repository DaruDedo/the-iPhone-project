Before campus starts, run through our ultimate back-to-college iPhone protection checklist to make sure your phone is safe:

1. **Edge Protection**: Choose a case with TPU bumpers and raised camera borders.
2. **Screen Safety**: Install 8K privacy tempered glass to keep your chats safe from classmates sitting next to you.
3. **MagSafe Wallet**: Carry your college ID and debit card on the back of your phone, eliminating a bulky wallet.
