Finding a decent case under ₹500 in India can be tough because the market is flooded with cheap, smelly plastic covers that turn yellow in a week. However, you can still find good protective options if you know what to look for.

Look for basic TPU bumper cases that offer raised corners and clicky buttons. Avoid cases with fake leather or bad painting, as they peel off in hot weather. A simple matte TPU cover is the best choice under ₹500 to protect your phone from scratches and light drops.
