The camera bump on modern iPhones is massive, making it highly exposed to scratches during flat drops. A camera-focused case design is a must-have.

Look for cases with a raised metal camera border of at least 1.2mm. This ensures that the glass lens elements never touch flat surfaces when you place your phone face down, protecting your lenses from scratches.
