Extremely cold temperatures can cause temporary battery drain or sudden shut-offs. To keep your battery running smoothly during winter:

Keep your phone in inner jacket pockets close to body heat. Avoid charging in freezing environments, and use protective cases with soft internal linings to insulate the battery.
