A single speck of dust on the sticky side of your tempered glass can ruin the installation. Fortunately, you can clean dust from screen guard adhesive easily:

Gently lift the protector, take a piece of clear sticky tape, and dab the sticky side against the glass adhesive. The tape will lift the dust particle off without damaging the silicone layer.
