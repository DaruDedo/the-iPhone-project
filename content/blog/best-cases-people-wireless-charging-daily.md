If you use wireless charging daily, a thick case is your enemy. It increases coil spacing, causing slow charging and high thermal throttling.

Choose cases with backplate thickness below 1.2mm and integrated magnetic alignment rings. This reduces induction energy waste, keeps your phone cool, and preserves battery life.
