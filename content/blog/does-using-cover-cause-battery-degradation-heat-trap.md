Excessive heat is the primary cause of battery health degradation. Does a phone case trap heat battery capacity negatively?

Yes, thick leather or heavy rugged cases can trap heat during intensive tasks like charging or gaming. 

To protect your battery, remove thick cases during long charging sessions, or choose thin, ventilated polycarbonate cases that allow natural heat dissipation.
