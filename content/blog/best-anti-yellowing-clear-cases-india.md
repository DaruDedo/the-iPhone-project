Yellowing is the number one issue with transparent clear cases. It is caused by UV light and sweat oxidising the TPU plastic.

To prevent this, look for clear cases using hard polycarbonate backplates combined with UV-resistant aliphatic TPU bumpers. Our transparent cases are treated with custom anti-yellowing agents to stay clear and fresh longer.
