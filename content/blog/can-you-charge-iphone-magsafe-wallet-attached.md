A common usability question is: can you charge your phone wirelessly while keeping your wallet snapped? Or does charging with magsafe wallet attached block the power flow?

The answer is: No, you cannot charge through the wallet. The steel shielding inside the wallet blocks magnetic induction to protect your cards. 

To charge, simply slide the wallet off, snap your MagSafe charger on, and slide the wallet back on when done.
