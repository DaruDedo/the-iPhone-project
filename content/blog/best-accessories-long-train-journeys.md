Long train journeys inside India require entertainment and constant battery power. To make your trip comfortable:

Use a privacy tempered glass so passengers sitting nearby cannot read your screen, carry a MagSafe power bank to charge wirelessly at your seat, and use cases with built-in loop mounts to prevent drops.
