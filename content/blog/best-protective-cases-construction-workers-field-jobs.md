Field engineering, construction sites, and outdoor industrial jobs are highly hazardous for glass smartphones. A simple thin case won't survive a drop onto gravel or steel beams.

For heavy-duty protection, choose rugged hybrid covers with dual-layer corner bumpers, dust-proof port flaps, and integrated camera bar protectors. This keeps sand, concrete dust, and impact force far away from your phone.
