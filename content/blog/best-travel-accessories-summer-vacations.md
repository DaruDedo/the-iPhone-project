Summer vacations involve long travel days, outdoor sightseeing, and capturing memories. Your travel setup should be compact and heat-resistant.

Ensure you use a thin polycarbonate case that allows your phone to dissipate heat quickly in warm weather, and carry a MagSafe power bank so you can charge wirelessly on the go without carrying tangled cables.
