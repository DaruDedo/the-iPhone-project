Have you ever installed a fresh tempered glass, only for your new back cover to push against the edges and peel it off? This happens when a screen protector is cut too close to the borders.

To prevent this, look for case friendly tempered glass models. These screen guards feature a fractional 0.5mm margin at the borders. This gap is invisible once installed but provides enough room for thick phone cases to wrap around the frame without lifting the glass.
