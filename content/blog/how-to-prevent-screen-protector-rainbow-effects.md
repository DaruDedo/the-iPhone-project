Rainbow oil slick patterns on your screen protector are caused by micro-gaps trapping air, or low-grade adhesive refracting light.

To prevent this:
1. Ensure the screen is completely dry and clean before installation.
2. Choose premium tempered glass with full static-absorption adhesive, which eliminates micro-gaps.
