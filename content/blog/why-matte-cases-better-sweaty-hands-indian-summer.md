During the peak of the Indian summer, high humidity makes your hands sweaty, turning glossy cases into slippery, greasy magnets for dust. A matte case sweat proof design is the perfect solution.

Frosted matte cases feature a micro-textured sandblasted backplate. This textured finish diffuses light, repels hand oils, and maintains a secure, non-sticky grip even during long calls in hot weather.
