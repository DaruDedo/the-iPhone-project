When traveling, you need convenience, durability, and less pocket clutter. A good travel case should support a modular MagSafe card wallet, allowing you to carry your hotel key and credit cards on the back of your phone.

Make sure the case bumper features textured non-slip rails to prevent drops during quick photography sessions, and has a raised camera lip to protect the lenses in transit.
