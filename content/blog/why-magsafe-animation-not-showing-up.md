When you snap an original MagSafe accessory, a cool circular animation shows up on your screen. If yours is missing, read our magsafe case animation fix steps:

The animation requires an NFC chip inside the accessory. Many third-party cases omit this chip. If your case has the chip, clean the back plate and restart your iPhone to fix the issue.
