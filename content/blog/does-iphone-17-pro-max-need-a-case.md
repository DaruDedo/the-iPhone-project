Apple builds the iPhone 17 Pro Max with aerospace-grade titanium and their latest Ceramic Shield front glass. It is tough, no doubt. But does that mean you can run it naked without a case? 

Let's talk facts. While titanium is highly scratch-resistant, the internal back-glass remains glass. In a real drop test, a titanium edge drop will not shatter the rail, but the shock waves travel directly through the frame, shattering the rear glass panel. A replacement for the rear back glass can cost a bomb in India.

For real iPhone 17 Pro Max drop protection, a premium thin case is the perfect middle ground. Our Frosted Air series offers a raised 1.2mm camera lip and reinforced corners that absorb force, ensuring your device stays sleek and safe. If you want to show off the premium look without risking a shattered screen, running a quality case is non-negotiable.
