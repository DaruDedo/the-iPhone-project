The worst thing about cheap cases is mushy buttons that require hard presses. That is why choosing cases with aluminum buttons is highly recommended.

CNC-machined aluminum buttons are separate inserts that float inside the TPU border. When pressed, they deliver a crisp tactile snap, replicating the click feel of your native iPhone buttons.
