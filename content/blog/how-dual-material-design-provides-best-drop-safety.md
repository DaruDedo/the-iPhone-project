A single-material case is always a compromise. Hard cases don't absorb shock, and soft cases slip off. A hybrid case protection design offers the perfect solution.

By fusing a flexible TPU bumper (for corner drop shock absorption) with a rigid polycarbonate backplate (to prevent twisting and back-glass damage), you get optimal, balanced protection.
