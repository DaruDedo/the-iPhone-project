If your wireless charging speed drops suddenly, it is usually caused by heat. As the phone gets warm, its battery protection systems throttle charging to protect battery health.

Ensure you use cases with built-in magnetic alignment rings to lock the coils center-to-center, reducing heat and maintaining optimal speeds.
