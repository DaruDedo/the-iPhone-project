Using harsh household chemicals (like bleach or glass cleaner) can strip the oleophobic coating off your iPhone screen. 

The safest way to disinfect is using a microfiber cloth lightly dampened with 70% isopropyl alcohol. Avoid getting moisture in the charging ports or speaker grills.
