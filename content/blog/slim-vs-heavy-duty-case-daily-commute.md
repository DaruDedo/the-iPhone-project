Choosing between a slim vs shockproof case is a daily dilemma for urban commuters. You want protection from crowded train rides and drop hazards, but you also want a phone that fits into your pocket.

* **Heavy-Duty Cases**: Offer massive drop protection but add bulk and weight. They make single-handed text messaging difficult.
* **Slim Cases**: Keep the phone compact and stylish but can lack drop protection if made from cheap materials.

Our Frosted cases solve this by using a hybrid structure: soft shock-absorbing TPU bumpers on the edges paired with a rigid, ultra-thin polycarbonate backplate, offering the best of both worlds.
