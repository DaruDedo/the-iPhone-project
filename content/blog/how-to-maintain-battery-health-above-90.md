To keep your battery health above 90% after a year of use, follow these core charging habits:

1. **Avoid extreme heat**: Remove thick cases while charging.
2. **Use MagSafe**: Perfect magnetic alignment reduces inductive heat waste.
3. **Optimized Charging**: Keep your battery charge between 20% and 80% whenever possible.
