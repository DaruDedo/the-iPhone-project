If you have to squeeze your phone case with massive force just to adjust the volume, the case is poorly engineered. Stiff button covers occur when cases use molded-plastic bumpers.

Premium cases resolve this by using independent button cutouts and inserting CNC-machined metal keys, delivering a satisfying, responsive click feel.
