Many fitness enthusiasts wonder if they can use their MagSafe case to mount their phone directly to gym racks. Will a magnetic case gym mount hold up?

Yes! Because cases have embedded magnets, they will stick to flat steel surfaces like gym racks, allowing you to record workouts or check routines easily.
