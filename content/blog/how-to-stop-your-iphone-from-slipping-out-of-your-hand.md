Modern iPhones are made of smooth glass and polished metals, making them highly slippery. To prevent accidental drops, you need a case with secure grip design.

Look for covers that feature micro-textured side rails or soft-touch matte backplates. These materials increase surface friction against your palm, ensuring a comfortable, non-slip grip.
