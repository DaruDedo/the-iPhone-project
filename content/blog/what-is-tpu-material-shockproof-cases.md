Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) is the industry standard for shockproof cases. Let's compare tpu vs polycarbonate case behaviors to see why they are paired together.

* **TPU (Elastic Bumper)**: TPU is highly flexible, rubber-like, and absorbs shock waves. When your phone drops, the TPU bumper compresses, dispersing the impact energy before it reaches the frame.
* **Polycarbonate (Rigid Shield)**: Polycarbonate is a hard, rigid plastic that prevents sharp objects from piercing the back glass of the phone.

Pairing a TPU bumper with a polycarbonate back plate creates a highly effective hybrid protective cover.
