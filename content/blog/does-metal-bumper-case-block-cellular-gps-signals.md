Metal cases look sleek, but do metal cases block signal bands? 

Yes. Aluminum is a conductor that acts as a Faraday cage, blocking electromagnetic waves. This can cause dropped calls, slow cellular data, and inaccurate GPS navigation. 

To avoid signal issues, choose premium plastic, polycarbonate, or hybrid TPU cases.
