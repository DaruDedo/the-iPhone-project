Phones get warm under heavy gaming or video recording. A thick rubber case traps this heat. A cooling phone case design focuses on thermal conductivity.

Our frosted polycarbonate cases are designed with a thin 1.1mm backplate. Polycarbonate has better thermal transfer properties than thick silicone, allowing heat to escape quickly and keeping your processor running smoothly.
