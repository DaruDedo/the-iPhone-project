While modern iPhones have water resistance, rainwater can still corrode charging ports and speakers. During the Indian monsoon:

1. Use cases with a secure, non-slip textured grip to prevent the phone from slipping out of wet hands.
2. Shake out any water from the speaker grills immediately.
3. Never plug in a charging cable if the port is damp; let it dry naturally.
