The camera bump on modern iPhones is highly exposed. Is using a lens protector worth it, or does it degrade photo clarity?

High-quality camera lens protectors are made from ultra-high transparency glass that preserves 100% light transmission, causing zero glare. They act as cheap insurance, protecting your expensive camera glass from direct scratch and crack hazards.
