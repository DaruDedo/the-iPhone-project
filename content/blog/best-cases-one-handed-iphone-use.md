Texting with one hand on large iPhones can cause thumb strain and sudden drops. For easy single-handed typing, your case must be slim and grippy.

Choose cases with micro-textured bumpers that provide a secure hand grip. Keep the case thickness minimal so your thumb can easily reach across the entire screen without stretching.
