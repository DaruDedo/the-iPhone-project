Apple’s official silicone cases look beautiful and fit perfectly, but they cost a steep ₹4,900 in India. Let's compare them against premium third-party alternatives:

* **Official Apple Silicone**: Offers perfect fit, soft-touch silicone, microfiber lining, and official MagSafe animation. However, the silicone can peel at the corners and attracts pocket lint.
* **Premium Third-Party Cases**: Offer the same hybrid drop safety, clicky aluminum buttons, and N52 magnets for a fraction of the cost (around ₹799 - ₹1500).

Unless you absolutely need the Apple logo on your case, third-party covers offer much better value for your money.
