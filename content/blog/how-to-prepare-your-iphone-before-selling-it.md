Before handing over your old iPhone to a buyer:

1. Back up your data to iCloud.
2. Deregister your Apple Watch.
3. Sign out of iCloud and Erase All Content and Settings.
4. Clean the phone frame and remove any old screen protectors to showcase its mint condition.
