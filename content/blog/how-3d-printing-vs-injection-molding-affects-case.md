While 3D printing is great for quick prototyping, it is unsuitable for protective cases. Let's look at injection molded case quality benefits.

3D prints have layer lines that create weak shear points, cracking easily under stress. 

High-pressure injection molding creates a single, homogeneous polymer piece. This ensures uniform density, smooth finishes, and maximum structural strength to absorb drop impacts.
