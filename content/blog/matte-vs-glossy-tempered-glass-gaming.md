Mobile gamers need maximum touch response and zero finger friction. Traditional glossy screen guards can feel sticky when fingers get sweaty, which is why checking out matte tempered glass for gaming is popular.

* **Glossy Glass**: Delivers maximum screen color contrast and clarity but can attract fingerprints and feel sticky.
* **Matte Glass**: Features a micro-etched textured finish that diffuses light reflection and allows fingers to slide smoothly. It is the best choice for fast-paced gaming, though it can slightly reduce screen contrast.
