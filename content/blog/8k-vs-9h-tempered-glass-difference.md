If you are shopping for a screen guard, you have probably seen terms like "9H Hardness" and "8K Premium Glass". What do they actually mean?

* **9H Hardness**: This refers to the pencil hardness scale. A 9H rated glass cannot be scratched by anything softer than a 9H hardness mineral (like keys or coins). Almost all quality tempered glass is 9H rated.
* **8K Premium Glass**: This is a term used for ultra-high clarity aluminosilicate glass. It features double-tempered treatment and superior polishing, ensuring that pixel visibility remains crystal clear without any rainbow effects or distortion.
