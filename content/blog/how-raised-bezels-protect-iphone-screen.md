When placing your phone face down on a desk or counter, the screen is exposed to dirt and micro-scratches. That is why raised lip case protection is the most critical design feature.

A raised bezel lip of 1.1mm to 1.3mm acts as a physical barrier. It keeps the glass screen hovering safely above flat surfaces, ensuring that grit, sand, and dust never touch your screen.
