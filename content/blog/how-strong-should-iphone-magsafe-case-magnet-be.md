A MagSafe case magnet needs to be strong, but not too strong. If it is too weak, accessories slip off. If it is too strong, removing the phone from a desk stand becomes a two-handed struggle.

The optimal magsafe magnet pull strength is between 800g and 1100g. This provides a secure snap while allowing easy detachment when tilted.
