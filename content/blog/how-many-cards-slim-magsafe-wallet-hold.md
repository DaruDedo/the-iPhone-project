A slim card holder wallet is designed to help you declutter. But how many cards can it actually hold without bulging?

Most premium MagSafe wallets are engineered to hold exactly 2 to 3 cards (such as your ID, primary credit card, and metro card). 

Attempting to force a fourth card can stretch the elastic tension bands or leather, making the cards loose. For the best experience, keep your carry minimal.
