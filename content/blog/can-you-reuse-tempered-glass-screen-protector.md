If you misaligned your screen protector during installation, can you lift and reuse it? The answer is: Yes, but only immediately.

Reusing screen protector adhesive works if the static layer hasn't accumulated dust. Gently lift the glass, blow away any dust particles, and realign it immediately. Avoid doing this if the adhesive layer has already dried or bent.
