Did you know that dust particles trapped inside your case can scratch the back glass of your phone over time? A case with microfiber lining is the perfect solution.

The soft microfiber layer cushion traps microscopic dust particles within its fibers, preventing them from rubbing against the phone’s glass back during daily vibration and movement.
