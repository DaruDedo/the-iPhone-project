Clear polycarbonate is impact-resistant but soft enough to be scratched by pocket keys. An anti scratch clear case utilizes a sub-micron protective layer.

We cure our clear panels with a custom UV-hardened silica coating. This hard layer increases the surface resistance to 4H hardness, keeping your clear case crystal clear and scratch-free.
