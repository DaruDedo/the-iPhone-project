Carrying a heavy traditional wallet in your pocket is uncomfortable. A modular card holder cover is the modern solution.

By combining a slim MagSafe wallet with your phone case, you can carry your essential cards (like license and debit card) snapped directly to the back of your phone, keeping your daily pocket load light.
