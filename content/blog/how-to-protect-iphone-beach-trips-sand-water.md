A trip to the beach is fun, but sand particles are highly abrasive and can scratch your screen or clog the speaker ports. You need a reliable dust proof iphone case setup.

Ensure your case has precision speaker grill cutouts and fits tightly around the buttons. Avoid removing the case on the sand, and use port plugs to keep sea spray and dust out of the charging port.
