It is tempting to buy a 150 INR case from a local market, but let's review the cheap cover dangers. 

Cheap cases use hard recycled plastics that can scratch the metal rails of your phone during installation. They lack shock-absorbing bumpers and camera lips, leaving your screen highly exposed to shattering in a drop.
